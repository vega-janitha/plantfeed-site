# Solution is here...

PlantFEED is an automated plant-feeding device designed for home gardens, indoor plantations, greenhouses, and outdoor environments. It delivers the perfect balance of water and nutrients to your plants at the right time, all with zero hassle.

Connected to a drip irrigation system, PlantFEED ensures precise and efficient watering, minimizing waste while keeping your plants healthy and thriving.

The device operates fully autonomously, providing consistent care even when you're busy or away. You can also override or trigger watering at any time using the mobile app or the built-in control buttons on the machine.

# Technical Specifications

*   **Product Name:** PlantFEED
*   **Model Name:** PlantFEED-Home
*   **Model Number:** VG25001P
*   **Power Source:** 230V 50Hz
*   **Power Rating:** 65W, max at operation
*   **Idle Power Rating:** <5W
*   **Dimensions (mm):**
    *   Main Unit: 560 × 360 × 120
    *   With 40L external bucket: 560 × 360 × 420
*   **Connectivity Options:** 2.4 GHz Wi-Fi
*   **Operation Modes:**
    *   Scheduled automation (via app)
    *   Manual control (via app or physical buttons)
*   **Required water inlet pressure:** 0.02 MPa-0.8 MPa
*   **Dripping ends support:** 100
*   **Fertilizer flow rate:** 100 ml per minute
*   **Country of Origin:** Sri Lanka
